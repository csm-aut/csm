Minimal hardware requirements for migration:
  1. All RSP's and RP's are supported in ASR9K-64 and are in IOS XR RUN state
  2. All FC's are supported in ASR9K-64 and are in OK state.
  3. All supported LC's are in IOS XR RUN state.
  4. All supported FAN/PEM's are in READY state.
By default, on top of the minimal hardware requirements, the hardware audit checks that all FAN's and PEM's are supported in ASR9K-64.
While you can skip the hardware audit for FAN's and PEM's, note that migration may fail for unsupported LC's, FAN's and PEM's.
